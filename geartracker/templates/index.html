About Gear Tracker

Gear Tracker helps you manage any component that is suseptible to wear and tear. Bike components will eventually wear out or fail. Knowing when that happens can be a challenge.

With Gear Tracker, your components are tracked based on mileage, elevation and type of use. Using a proven algorithm those factors Gear Tracker can help predict when your components should be replaced to help reduce maintenance and down time.

If you're a dealer, service shop or retailer, you can also benefit from licensing our technology to help your customers stay on top of their maintenance.
